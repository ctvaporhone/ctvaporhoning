What is Vapor Honing?

Vapor honing is a wet blasting process that uses water, abrasive media, and air to clean and refine surfaces. It’s ideal for restoring parts without causing damage, leaving a smooth, satin-like finish suitable for a wide range of materials.

About

What began as a hobby restoring old tools with a small, entry-level sandblasting cabinet quickly evolved into something more. Though starting a business had long been a goal, we weren’t sure where to begin—until we discovered vapor blasting. After extensive research, the advantages became clear: superior surface finishes, improved operator safety, and a more environmentally friendly process compared to traditional dry blasting. Today, we’re a fully insured LLC operating the largest commercially available American-made wet blasting cabinet on the market.
